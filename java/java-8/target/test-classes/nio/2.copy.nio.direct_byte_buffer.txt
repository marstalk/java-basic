《静夜思》 - 李白

床前明月光，疑是地上霜。
举头望明月，低头思故乡。

English======

"Calm Night Thoughts" - Li Bai

The bright moonlight before my bed, I suspect it to be frost on the ground.
I raise my head to gaze at the shining moon, and lower it, missing my hometown.
